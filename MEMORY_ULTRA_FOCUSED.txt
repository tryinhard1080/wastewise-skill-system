WasteWise Complete Suite is a comprehensive waste analysis platform branded as "WasteWise by THE Trash Hub" (never mention Advantage Waste in deliverables). Delivers Excel workbook (9 tabs) and HTML dashboard (6 tabs) with executive summary.

Excel workbook structure (9 tabs): 
1. SUMMARY_FULL - Starts with "Potential to Reduce 2026 Trash Expense by $X,XXX"
2. EXPENSE_ANALYSIS - Row-based format where each invoice appears as separate row with columns: Month | Vendor | Service Type | Invoice Number | Amount | Cost/Door | Notes. Includes monthly subtotals with budget per door and grand total with average monthly budget
3. HAUL_LOG - Compactor pickup details (only created if compactor detected)
4. OPTIMIZATION - Three opportunities with full calculation breakdowns
5. CONTRACT_TERMS - Risk analysis with calendar reminders (only created if contract provided)
6. REGULATORY_COMPLIANCE - Orlando-style format with professional tables, color-coded status indicators (VERIFY=orange, RECOMMENDED=blue), priority levels (HIGH=red, MEDIUM=orange, LOW=green). Sections include: Overview, Mandatory Requirements, Compliance Checklist, Licensed Haulers, Penalties, Sources. Requires minimum 3-5 licensed haulers with confidence scoring (HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW)
7. LEASE-UP_NOTES - Lease-up phase assessment (only created when property generates >40% below benchmark indicating not at stabilized occupancy)
8. DOCUMENTATION_NOTES - Vendor contacts extracted from invoices and calculation formulas
9. QUALITY_CHECK - Validation summary showing results of 40+ pre-output checks
